Problem 2: Transfer claim from unexempted establishment to unexempted est is settled but amount is not reflected
  • Process The transfer claim has to be initiated by transfer out(old) office and after successful settlement the transfer In is reflected in office pertaining to new account. The transfer in claim has to be processed by new office and the transfer In claim has to be approved. The status shown in Claim status is only the status of Transfer Out claim of previous office. There is a system which checks if the details of transfer in and transfer out claims office are matching, if it is not matching, after settlement of claim at each office the Claim will be forwarded to special role to verify the mismatch.
  • Scenarios Due to this many scenarios are possible in trasfer cases where the claim will be showing as settled but amount will not be credited: 1. Trasfer Out settled but pending at Transfer In office
    2. Transfer Out settled but Rejected by Transfer In office
    3. Transfer Out settled and settled by Transfer In office
    4. Transfer Out Settled but sent to verify mismatch in Transfer Out office
    5. Transfer Out Settled, verified mismatch and settled by Transfer In office but sent to Transfer In office
  • Outcome There is no member dashboard to show the status of claim in transfer in office and becomes a reason for grievance
  • Reasons for rejection/mismatch
    1. Difference in basic details such as Name, DOB, Gender, Fathers name
    2. DOJ and DOE Overlap
    3. Uneven contribution
    4. EPS membership different in two account
    5. Different rate of contribution in EPS in two accounts
  • How to avoid:
    1. Make sure basic details are matching in both accounts
    2. Make sure DOJ and DOE for every past service is updated and correct
    3. make sure if there is uneven contribution NCP days are updated
    4. EPS membership should be uniform as per eligibility
    5. Rate of contribution should be consistant across services
